Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Summary: FocusClock respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to FocusClock ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application FocusClock (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Data Stored Locally

FocusClock stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Focus session history (start time, end time, duration, completion status)
  • App settings and preferences (sound, vibration, notifications, language)
  • Statistics and achievements data

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Any data that could identify you personally

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Advertising

FocusClock displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect certain data for ad personalization as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

We have configured ads to be non-personalized where possible. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

3.2 No Other Third-Party Services

We do not use any other third-party analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services.

4. Data Storage and Security

All your focus session data and settings are stored locally on your device using SQLite database. This data is:

  • Stored only on your device
  • Not backed up to any cloud service by our App
  • Deleted when you uninstall the App

5. Children's Privacy

FocusClock is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since we don't collect personal data from any user, this applies to users of all ages.

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data:

  • Access: View your data anytime within the App
  • Delete: Clear your data by uninstalling the App
  • Portability: Your data stays on your device

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: focusclock.app@gmail.com

In Simple Terms: FocusClock is a privacy-friendly app. Your focus data stays on your phone, we don't track you, and we don't sell any data because we don't collect any.
